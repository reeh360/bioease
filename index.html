The Science Behind Nerve Pain: Why Most Solutions Fail

To understand why NerveCalm might work where others fail, we need to examine the complexity of neuropathic pain. Unlike typical pain caused by injury or inflammation, nerve pain originates from damaged or malfunctioning nerves themselves.

Research published in the Journal of Pain Research indicates that effective neuropathy treatment requires a multi-target approach, addressing:

  1. Nerve inflammation and oxidative stress
  2. Nerve conduction abnormalities
  3. Neurotransmitter imbalances
  4. The blood-nerve barrier integrity

Most over-the-counter solutions only address one of these factors, if any. Prescription medications often come with significant side effects that make long-term use challenging.
